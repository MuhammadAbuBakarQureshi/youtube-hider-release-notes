YouTube Hider Release Notes

All updates and improvements for every version.

v1.1.0 - 2025-12-25 Latest
  • Shorts Update: The scroll-down button is now hidden, and the Shorts shelf is removed from horizontal video feeds.
  • Feed Logic: "Hide Home Feed" no longer affects your channel pages or individual playlist views.
  • Universal Comments: The comment hider now works seamlessly across both standard videos and Shorts.
v1.0.0 - 2025-11-30
  • Initial Release: Official launch of the YouTube Hider extension.
  • Core Features: Added toggle switches for Home Feed, Shorts, Recommended Videos, and Playlists.
  • Interaction Controls: Added visibility toggles for Live Chat and Comments.
  • Optimized UI: Dark-themed dashboard designed to integrate with the YouTube ecosystem.
